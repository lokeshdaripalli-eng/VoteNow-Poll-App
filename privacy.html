Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Data Collection

We believe in minimal data collection. We do not store your name, email, or any personal identification. We only store the votes you cast and use local browser storage to prevent duplicate voting.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

VoteNow uses `localStorage` to keep track of which polls you have already participated in. This data never leaves your browser and is only used to provide a fair polling experience.

3. Data Sharing

Since we don't collect personal data, we have nothing to sell or share with third parties. Your opinions are yours alone.

4. Contact

For any privacy concerns, feel free to reach out to the creator.
